About Me

Hi, I'm Yuu. I'm passionate about arts and specialize illustrations and graphic designs.

With 6 years of drawing digital art experience and 8 years of traditional art experience, I've worked on projects ranging from line arts, chibi and celluloid styles to realistic and impasto styles. I focus more on the color tones, line arts and details of a work to make sure it achieves the effect I want.

I specialize in 2d-style illustrations but am also skilled in photography, UI/UX design, 3D modeling, makeup and character design.

When I'm not working, I offer art commissions for personal and business used projects. Feel free to reach out for creative needs and custom commissions!
