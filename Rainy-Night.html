Concept and Planning

I'm working on concept pieces featuring my original character - K ("Kou Yuu"). For this artwork, I want to tell a story about a lone figure walking into a raining alley.

The scene is drawn from a low-angle perspective and some use of color to emphasize the character's movement and highlight key details, such as the umbrella, a road sign, the alley and puddles reflecting the glow of nearby lights.
